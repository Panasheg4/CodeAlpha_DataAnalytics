The 5 Questions Framework:
Every time you get a new dataset ask:
1. What IS this data?

How many rows/columns?
What does each column mean?

2. Is it CLEAN?

Missing values?
Duplicates?
Wrong data types?

3. What's NORMAL?

What's the average?
What's typical behaviour?

4. What's UNUSUAL?

Outliers?
Missing years?
Sudden jumps?

5. What RELATIONSHIPS exist?

Does X affect Y?
Correlations?
